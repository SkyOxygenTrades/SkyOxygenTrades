Our Specialties

Refurbished Materials: At Barnacle KGJ Inc., we offer a selection of refurbished materials backed by our extensive industry experience spanning over three decades. Our team carefully selects and inspects each item to ensure its quality and reliability. We understand the importance of providing cost-effective solutions without compromising on quality. If we're confident in the quality, we'll offer refurbished materials, but if not, we'll guide you to where you can purchase new items. Our goal is simple: to save you money while providing dependable products for your needs.

Custom Solutions: Whether you're a trade school in need of welding supplies or equipment or a manufacturing business seeking custom-designed solutions, we've got you covered. Our team of experts is dedicated to understanding your unique requirements and delivering tailor-made solutions that exceed your expectations.

Bid Assistance, Contractor Procurement, and Lead Generation: In addition to our core services, we offer comprehensive support to help you secure projects and contracts for future business endeavors. Our bid assistance services encompass the entire bidding process, from proposal preparation to contract negotiations. We leverage our industry connections and expertise to increase your chances of winning bids and securing lucrative projects. Furthermore, we specialize in contractor procurement, where we seek out skilled contractors to meet the work needs of our customers. Whether it's finding specialized installers or skilled contractors, we connect you with trusted professionals who ensure your projects are completed with precision and efficiency. In exchange for our services, we may receive a commission or finder's fee. Additionally, our lead generation services help you identify and pursue promising business opportunities in the welding industry.
